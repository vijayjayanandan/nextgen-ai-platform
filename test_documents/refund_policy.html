Government Service Refund Policy

Overview

This document outlines the refund policy for government services and fees.

Refund Policy

Refunds for government service fees are available under the following circumstances:

  • Service Not Provided: Full refund if the requested service was not delivered
  • Processing Errors: Full refund for errors made by government staff
  • Duplicate Payments: Refund of duplicate charges within 30 days
  • Cancelled Applications: Partial refund (minus processing fee) if application is cancelled before processing begins

Refund Process

  1. Submit refund request form within 90 days of payment
  2. Provide original receipt and supporting documentation
  3. Allow 4-6 weeks for processing
  4. Refund will be issued to original payment method

Exceptions

Refunds are not available for:

  • Completed services that meet quality standards
  • Applications rejected due to incomplete information provided by applicant
  • Services requested more than 1 year ago

Contact Information

For refund inquiries, contact the Finance Department at refunds@gov.ca or call 1-800-REFUND-1.
